# Dark Mode

Minutes supports three theme modes:

| Mode | Behavior |
|------|----------|
| **System** (default) | Follows your OS preference |
| **Dark** | Always dark |
| **Light** | Always light |

## Toggle

Click the theme button in the header (moon/sun/monitor icon) to cycle through modes. Or press `D` on your keyboard.

Your preference is saved to localStorage.
